## ⚠️ LEGAL COMPLIANCE — READ THIS FIRST, EVERY TIME

Lisa is a **licensed Registered Nurse**. Wrong words can risk her license and violate FTC/FDA wellness advertising guidelines.

### NEVER USE:
- `heals` / `healing` / `heal`
- `cures` / `cure`
- `treats` / `treatment` (in a medical/clinical sense)
- `diagnoses` / `diagnosis`
- Any definitive claim that PEMF *will* fix a specific condition
- "therapy" used to imply medical treatment (use "session" or "wellness service")

### ALWAYS USE INSTEAD:
- `supports` / `may support`
- `promotes comfort` / `promotes relaxation`
- `clients report feeling...`
- `designed to promote wellness`
- `restorative` / `restorative experience`
- `non-invasive wellness service`
- `calming session`
- `may help with discomfort`
- `support the body's natural processes`

### Required Site Disclaimers:
- "PEMF is a wellness service, not a medical treatment."
- "Not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or condition."
- "Results may vary. Individual experiences differ."
- Contraindications must be listed on the site (see PRD).

### Testimonials Rule:
- Clients can describe their own personal experiences in their own words ✅
- We do NOT add editorial spin that makes medical claims ✅
- Acceptable: "I felt amazing after my session!" (client's words)
- Acceptable: "My SI pain was 90% reduced." (client's words, their claim)
- NOT acceptable: "PEMF healed her SI joint." (our claim)
